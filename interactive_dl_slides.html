üìã Three Types of Measurement Problems

üìà

1. Regression Problems

Task: Predict continuous numerical outcomes

Example: Locating street vendors in satellite images

  • Predict GPS coordinates
  • Measure economic activity
  • Output: (latitude, longitude)
üè∑Ô∏è

2. Classification Problems

Task: Assign to known, discrete categories

Example: Economic policy uncertainty detection

  • Classes are prespecified
  • Multi-class or multi-label variants
  • Output: Yes/No or Category A/B/C
üï∏Ô∏è

3. Embeddings/Unsupervised

Task: Discover relationships without prespecified classes

Use case: Exploring large datasets

  • Find patterns and topics
  • No predetermined categories
  • Work with vector representations

ü§î Which Problem Type?

The choice depends on:

  • Known categories: Classification
  • Continuous values: Regression
  • Unknown patterns: Embeddings

ü§î When might you choose embeddings over classification?
